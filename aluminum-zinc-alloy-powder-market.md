<p><p>The aluminum zinc alloy powder market is characterized by two main types: flake and spherical powders. Flake powders are produced through mechanical processes and are known for their large surface area, making them suitable for applications like coatings and surface treatments. Spherical powders, on the other hand, are created through atomization and exhibit better flow properties, which enhances their use in additive manufacturing and advanced aerospace applications. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
