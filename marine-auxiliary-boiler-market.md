<p><strong>The Marine Auxiliary Boiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Tube Type</li><li>Smoke Tube Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine Auxiliary Boiler market is primarily categorized into two types: Water Tube and Smoke Tube boilers. Water Tube Boilers utilize water-filled tubes to absorb heat from combustion gases, offering high efficiency and rapid steam generation, ideal for ships with varying demand. Smoke Tube Boilers, on the other hand, feature flue gases passing through tubes submerged in water, providing simpler construction and maintenance. Both types cater to different operational needs, with Water Tube preferred for larger vessels and Smoke Tube for smaller ships.</p></p>
