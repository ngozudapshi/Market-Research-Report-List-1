<p><p>The solid tumor therapeutics market includes various treatment modalities to address cancer. Chemotherapy employs cytotoxic drugs to kill rapidly dividing cells, while hormone therapy targets hormone-dependent tumors by blocking hormone production or action. Immunotherapy enhances the body's immune response against cancer cells, leveraging immune system activity. Targeted therapy focuses on specific genetic or molecular markers in tumors, aiming to disrupt cancer cell growth without harming normal cells. Each approach plays a crucial role in managing solid tumors effectively.</p></p>
