<p><strong>The Subsonic Wind Tunnel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Wind Tunnel</li><li>Closed Wind Tunnel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The subsonic wind tunnel market is primarily divided into open and closed wind tunnels. Open wind tunnels have an unobstructed flow path, allowing air to enter and exit freely, making them simpler and often more cost-effective for basic aerodynamic testing. Conversely, closed wind tunnels feature a controlled loop system that recirculates air, allowing for more precise measurements and consistent test conditions. Closed tunnels are typically used for advanced research and development, while open tunnels serve educational and initial testing purposes.</p></p>
