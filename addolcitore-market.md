<p><ul><li>Salt Based</li><li>Salt Free</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water softener market can be divided into salt-based and salt-free types. Salt-based softeners use ion exchange technology to remove calcium and magnesium ions, replacing them with sodium ions, effectively reducing water hardness. In contrast, salt-free softeners utilize alternative methods, such as template-assisted crystallization or magnetic conditioning, to alter the structure of hard minerals, preventing scale buildup without eliminating hardness. Each type caters to different consumer needs, balancing efficiency, maintenance, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
