<p><p>The Biaxially Oriented Nylon (BOPA) Film market is categorized primarily into two types based on stretching methods: Distributed Biaxial Stretching and Simultaneous Biaxial Stretching. Distributed Biaxial Stretching involves the sequential application of tension in both directions, leading to enhanced film properties through controlled stretching. In contrast, Simultaneous Biaxial Stretching applies tension in both directions simultaneously, resulting in uniform thickness and mechanical strength. Both methods contribute uniquely to the film's overall performance and suitability for various applications, such as packaging and industrial uses.</p></p>
