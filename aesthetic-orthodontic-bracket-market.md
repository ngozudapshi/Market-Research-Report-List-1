<p><strong>The Aesthetic Orthodontic Bracket Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Ligation</li><li>Self-Ligating Brackets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aesthetic orthodontic bracket market consists primarily of two types: conventional ligation brackets and self-ligating brackets. Conventional ligation brackets require elastic or steel ligatures to hold the archwire in place, providing a traditional approach to treatment. In contrast, self-ligating brackets utilize a built-in mechanism to secure the archwire, allowing for reduced friction and quicker adjustments. Both types aim to enhance patient comfort and aesthetics while effectively aligning teeth, catering to varying preferences and treatment needs in orthodontics.</p></p>
