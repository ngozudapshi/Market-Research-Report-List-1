<p><strong>The Rubber Repair Glue Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Bond</li><li>Cold Bond</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber repair glue market is primarily divided into two types: hot bond and cold bond adhesives. Hot bond adhesives require heat application to activate and create a strong bond, making them ideal for high-stress repairs where durability is essential. In contrast, cold bond adhesives cure at room temperature, offering convenience and ease of use for less demanding applications. Each type caters to specific repair needs, with hot bond being favored for industrial settings, while cold bond is popular for quick, on-the-go fixes.</p></p>
