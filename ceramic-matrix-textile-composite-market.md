Low Bio-Persistent fibers feature reduced biological impact, making them safer while still providing adequate thermal resistance. Polycrystalline materials, characterized by their high strength and toughness, are utilized in demanding environments where durability and performance are critical. Together, they cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
