<p><ul><li>Halogen Type</li><li>Halogen Free Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire retardant polypropylene market is divided into two main types: halogen type and halogen-free type. Halogen-type fire retardants incorporate elements like chlorine or bromine, providing effective flame resistance at a lower cost but raising environmental and health concerns due to toxic smoke when burned. In contrast, halogen-free types utilize alternative compounds such as phosphorous, nitrogen, or mineral-based additives, offering a safer environmental profile while meeting fire safety standards. Both types serve diverse applications across various industries.</p></p>
