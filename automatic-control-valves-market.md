<p><p>The automatic control valves market includes several types, each serving different applications. Angle seat piston valves provide efficient flow control in compact spaces. Globe valves offer precise regulation, ideal for throttling services. Butterfly valves are lightweight and suitable for large pipe systems, allowing quick on-off operations. Ball valves provide tight sealing and fast operation, making them versatile for various fluids. Pinch valves, with flexible bodies, manage slurries effectively, while diaphragm valves ensure leak-free handling of corrosive fluids in sanitary applications.</p></p>
