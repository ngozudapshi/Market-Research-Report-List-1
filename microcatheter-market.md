<p><ul><li>Cardiovascular</li><li>Neurovascular</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microcatheter market is segmented into three primary types: cardiovascular, neurovascular, and others. Cardiovascular microcatheters are used in procedures involving the heart and blood vessels, facilitating interventions like angioplasty and stent placement. Neurovascular microcatheters target the brain's vascular system, aiding in treatments for conditions such as aneurysms and arteriovenous malformations. The "others" category encompasses various applications in areas like oncology and peripheral vascular interventions, highlighting the versatility of microcatheters in advanced medical procedures.</p></p>
