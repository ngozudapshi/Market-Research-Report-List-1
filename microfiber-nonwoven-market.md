<p><p>Microfiber nonwoven is a type of fabric made from ultrafine synthetic fibers, typically polyester or polyamide, which are bonded together mechanically, chemically, or thermally without weaving or knitting. These materials are known for their high absorbency, durability, softness, and ability to trap dust and dirt, making them ideal for applications in cleaning products, personal care, medical textiles, and filtration systems.</p><p>The Microfiber Nonwoven Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand across various industries such as healthcare, automotive, and hygiene products.
