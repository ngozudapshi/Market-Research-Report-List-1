<p><ul><li>Batch Sterilization</li><li>Continuous Sterilization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The retort machines market comprises two primary types: batch sterilization and continuous sterilization. Batch sterilization involves processing a specific quantity of products in a single cycle, ideal for smaller operations or varied product lines. Continuous sterilization, on the other hand, allows for a constant flow of products through the sterilization system, enhancing efficiency and throughput in larger operations. Both methods aim to preserve food safety and extend shelf life, but they cater to different production needs and scales.</p></p>
