<p><p>The organic fertilizer market comprises three main types: plant-based, animal-based, and mineral-based fertilizers. Plant-based fertilizers are derived from organic materials like compost, green manure, and plant residues, enriching soil with nutrients. Animal-based fertilizers include manure and byproducts from livestock, which enhance soil fertility and structure. Mineral-based organic fertilizers come from naturally occurring minerals and are utilized for their nutrient content. Together, these categories promote sustainable agriculture by improving soil health, crop yield, and environmental quality.</p></p>
