<p><p>The dental whitening strip market comprises two main types: gel whitestrips and dry whitestrips. Gel whitestrips are coated with a whitening gel that adheres to teeth, typically containing peroxide for effective stain removal. They offer a flexible fit and often provide more comfort. In contrast, dry whitestrips are pre-dried, thin, and might be less messy, but may require longer application times. Both types aim to enhance dental aesthetics by reducing discoloration, catering to various consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
