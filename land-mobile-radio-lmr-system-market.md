<p><ul><li>Analog</li><li>Digital</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Land Mobile Radio (LMR) system market is primarily divided into two types: analog and digital. Analog LMR systems transmit voice signals in continuous waveforms, offering simplicity and ease of use, but limited features and capacity. In contrast, digital LMR systems utilize discrete signals, allowing for enhanced voice clarity, increased spectrum efficiency, and advanced functionalities such as encryption and data services. The shift toward digital systems is driven by the need for improved communication quality and interoperability in various industries.</p></p>
