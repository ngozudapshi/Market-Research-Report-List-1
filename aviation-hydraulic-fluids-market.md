<p><p>The aviation hydraulic fluids market includes various types. Mineral-based hydraulic oils, the most common type, are petroleum-derived and provide reliable performance under high pressure and temperature. Phosphate-based hydraulic oils are engineered for high fire resistance and are often used in military aviation applications. Other specialty hydraulic fluids may include synthetics or biodegradable options, catering to specific aviation needs and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
