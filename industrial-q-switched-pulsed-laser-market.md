<p><strong>The Industrial Q-switched Pulsed Laser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Active Q-switching"</li><li>"Passive Q-switching"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Q-switched pulsed laser market is primarily segmented into two types: active Q-switching and passive Q-switching. Active Q-switching involves the use of external modulators, like acousto-optic or electro-optic devices, to control the laser output, allowing for precise timing and energy control. In contrast, passive Q-switching utilizes saturable absorbers within the laser cavity to achieve similar effects without external control. Both methods enable high peak power and short pulse durations, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
