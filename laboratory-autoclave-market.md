<p><strong>The Laboratory Autoclave Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Displacement Autoclave</li><li>Pre-vacuum Autoclave</li><li>Steam Flush Autoclave</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laboratory autoclave market consists of various types catering to different sterilization needs. Gravity displacement autoclaves employ gravity to allow steam to displace air, ensuring effective sterilization. Pre-vacuum autoclaves utilize a vacuum to remove air before steam penetration, enhancing efficiency for porous loads. Steam flush autoclaves combine rapid steam pulses for effective air removal and sterilization. Other autoclaves include types like microwave and ethylene oxide, which offer specialized solutions for various materials and applications in laboratories.</p></p>
