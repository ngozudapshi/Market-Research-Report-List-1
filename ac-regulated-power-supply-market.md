<p><p>The AC Regulated Power Supply market is divided into two main types: Non-Contact and Contact AC Regulated Power Supplies. Non-Contact AC Regulated Power Supplies utilize electromagnetic or transformer-based methods to maintain output voltage without direct electrical connection, ensuring safety and reducing wear. In contrast, Contact AC Regulated Power Supplies rely on direct electrical links, offering precise voltage regulation for critical applications. Both types cater to different industry needs, providing reliability and stability in power delivery for various electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
