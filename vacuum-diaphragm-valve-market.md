<p><p>The vacuum diaphragm valve market is segmented into three types based on the level of vacuum they handle. Low vacuum valves are designed for applications up to approximately 1 torr, suitable for basic industrial processes. High vacuum valves operate effectively in the range of 10^-3 to 10^-7 torr, commonly used in research and semiconductor industries. Ultra-high vacuum valves serve environments below 10^-7 torr, essential for advanced scientific applications like particle accelerators and space simulation. Each type caters to specific operational needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
