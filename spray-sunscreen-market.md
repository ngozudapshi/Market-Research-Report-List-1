<p><p>The spray sunscreen market features three primary types: physical, chemical, and a combination of both. Physical sunscreens contain active mineral ingredients like zinc oxide or titanium dioxide, creating a barrier that reflects UV rays. Chemical sunscreens use organic compounds to absorb UV radiation, transforming it into heat that the skin releases. The hybrid option combines both physical and chemical properties, offering broad-spectrum protection. Each type caters to different skin types and consumer preferences, impacting market demand and trends.</p></p>
