<p><p>The polysomnography market encompasses various types, including EEG, dynamic, and video polysomnography. EEG (electroencephalogram) monitors brain activity during sleep to diagnose sleep disorders. Dynamic polysomnography assesses changes in sleep patterns over time, offering insights into the evolution of sleep issues. Video polysomnography adds visual monitoring, allowing clinicians to observe patient movements and behaviors during sleep. Together, these types enhance the understanding and diagnosis of sleep disorders, enabling more effective treatment strategies for patients.</p></p>
