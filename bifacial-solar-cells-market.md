<p><strong>The Bifacial Solar Cells Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>N-Type</li><li>P-Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bifacial solar cells are categorized into N-Type and P-Type based on their doping materials. N-Type cells use phosphorus to create an excess of electrons, offering higher efficiency and better performance in low-light conditions. They are less susceptible to light-induced degradation. P-Type cells, doped with boron, are more common but have slightly lower efficiency and can experience more degradation over time. Both types leverage dual-sided light absorption, enhancing energy production and appealing to diverse market needs.</p></p>
