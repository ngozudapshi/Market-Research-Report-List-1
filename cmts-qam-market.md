<p><p>CMTS, or Cable Modem Termination System, is essential for cable internet services, using QAM (Quadrature Amplitude Modulation) to transmit data efficiently. The Integrated CMTS (I-CMTS) combines all functions in a single device, simplifying deployment but potentially limiting scalability. In contrast, the Modular CMTS (M-CMTS) offers flexibility through separate components, allowing operators to customize and scale their networks more easily. Both market types support broadband service delivery, but differ in architecture, scalability, and operational complexity.</p></p>
