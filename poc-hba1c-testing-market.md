<p><p>The POC HbA1C testing market comprises various types, including immunoassay, boronate affinity, and others. Immunoassay utilizes antibodies to detect glycated hemoglobin levels, providing rapid results. Boronate affinity methods rely on the specific binding of boronic acids to glycosylated hemoglobin, offering high accuracy. Other market types may include alternative technologies like enzymatic assays or microfluidic devices, which aim to deliver convenience and efficiency in diabetes management. Each type addresses the demand for quick and reliable monitoring of blood sugar levels.</p></p>
