<p><p>The horizontal film dryer market features three primary types: vacuum, normal pressure, and over pressure. Vacuum dryers operate under reduced pressure, enabling efficient drying of heat-sensitive materials with minimal thermal degradation. Normal pressure dryers function at atmospheric conditions, making them suitable for various applications where high drying rates are not critical. Over pressure dryers utilize elevated pressure to accelerate drying processes, often enhancing evaporation rates. Each type caters to specific material properties and operational requirements in industrial drying applications.</p></p>
