<p><p>The motorcycle brake caliper market features two main types: single piston and multi-piston calipers. Single piston calipers use one piston to apply even pressure on the brake pads, offering simplicity and lightweight design, ideal for smaller bikes. In contrast, multi-piston calipers incorporate multiple pistons to enhance braking performance, providing greater force and improved heat dissipation, making them suitable for high-performance motorcycles. Each type caters to different rider preferences and performance needs.</p></p>
