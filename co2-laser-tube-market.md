<p><ul><li>CO2 Glass Laser Tube</li><li>CO2 Radio Frequency Laser Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CO2 laser tube market is primarily divided into two types: CO2 glass laser tubes and CO2 radio frequency (RF) laser tubes. CO2 glass laser tubes are traditional, utilizing glass enclosures filled with a gas mixture to generate laser beams, commonly employed in cutting and engraving applications. In contrast, CO2 RF laser tubes use radio frequency excitation to produce a more efficient and stable beam, suited for higher power applications like industrial cutting and medical procedures. Both types have distinct advantages based on usage.</p></p>
