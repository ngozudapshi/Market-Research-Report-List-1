<p><p>The exoskeleton market is categorized into three main types: lower body, upper body, and full body exoskeletons. Lower body exoskeletons primarily assist in walking and mobility for individuals with lower limb disabilities, enhancing gait and stability. Upper body exoskeletons support the arms and shoulders, aiding rehabilitation or enhancing strength for tasks like lifting. Full body exoskeletons combine features of both, providing comprehensive support for users, often used in industrial applications and rehabilitation settings, improving overall mobility and functionality.</p></p>
