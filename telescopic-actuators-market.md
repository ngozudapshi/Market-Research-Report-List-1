<p><p>The telescopic actuators market is segmented into three main types: electric, hydraulic, and pneumatic actuators. Electric actuators utilize electric power for precise control and are commonly used in applications requiring accurate positioning. Hydraulic actuators rely on fluid pressure to generate motion, offering high force output suitable for heavy lifting tasks. Pneumatic actuators use compressed air to produce movement, favored for their quick response and lightweight nature. Each type serves distinct applications across various industries, such as manufacturing, construction, and robotics.</p></p>
