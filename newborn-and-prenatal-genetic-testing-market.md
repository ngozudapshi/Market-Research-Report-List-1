<p><strong>The Newborn and Prenatal Genetic Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PCR</li><li>FISH</li><li>aCGH</li><li>NIPT</li><li>MSS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The newborn and prenatal genetic testing market encompasses various diagnostic methods. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies DNA sequences for detecting genetic disorders. FISH (Fluorescence In Situ Hybridization) visualizes chromosomal abnormalities using fluorescent probes. aCGH (Array Comparative Genomic Hybridization) analyzes copy number variations across the genome for more detailed insights. NIPT (Non-Invasive Prenatal Testing) utilizes maternal blood to assess fetal genetic conditions without risk, while MSS (Maternal Serum Screening) evaluates maternal biomarkers to identify potential fetal anomalies. Each method serves distinct roles in early genetic assessment.</p></p>
