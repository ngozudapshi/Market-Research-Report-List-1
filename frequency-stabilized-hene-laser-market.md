<p><p>The Frequency Stabilized HeNe Laser market is divided into two primary types: Single Longitudinal Mode (SLM) and Dual Longitudinal Mode (DLM). SLM lasers emit light at a single resonant frequency, providing high stability and precision ideal for applications like metrology and spectroscopy. In contrast, DLM lasers operate with two closely spaced frequencies, which can enhance versatility in specific applications, allowing for broader tuning capabilities. Both types cater to differing needs across scientific and industrial sectors, driving their market growth.</p></p>
