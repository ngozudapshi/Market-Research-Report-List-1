<p><p>The Self-regulating Thermostatic Radiator Valves market comprises two main types: self-operated thermostatic radiator valves and electronic thermostatic radiator valves. Self-operated valves utilize a wax sensor that expands or contracts with temperature changes, automatically adjusting to regulate the flow of hot water based on room temperature. In contrast, electronic thermostatic radiator valves feature programmable settings and can be controlled remotely, allowing for precise temperature management and energy efficiency. Both types aim to enhance comfort and optimize heating efficiency in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
