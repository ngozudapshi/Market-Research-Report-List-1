<p><p>The low-density PET foam market is primarily segmented into two types: virgin PET and recycled PET. Virgin PET foam is produced from newly manufactured polyethylene terephthalate, offering superior performance, consistency, and thermal stability, making it ideal for high-demand applications. In contrast, recycled PET foam is sourced from post-consumer or industrial waste, promoting sustainability and reducing environmental impact. While recycled PET may have slightly lower mechanical properties, it provides a cost-effective and eco-friendly alternative, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers and industries.</p></p>
