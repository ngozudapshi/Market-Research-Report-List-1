<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Coating</li><li>UV Curing Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3C coating market comprises various types, primarily thermosetting and UV curing coatings. Thermosetting coatings are polymers that irreversibly harden when heated, providing durable, heat-resistant finishes ideal for automotive and industrial applications. On the other hand, UV curing coatings use ultraviolet light to quickly cure and harden, offering rapid production cycles and strong chemical resistance, making them popular in the electronics and printing sectors. Both types enhance product performance and longevity, catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>
