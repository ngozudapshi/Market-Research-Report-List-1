<p><p>The high-pressure release valves market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Spring Loaded Pressure Release Valves** utilize a spring mechanism to maintain pressure until a preset limit is reached, allowing controlled release.</p><p>2. **Pilot Operated Pressure Release Valves** use a smaller pilot valve to control larger flow, offering high accuracy and responsiveness.</p><p>3. **Dead Weight Pressure Release Valves** rely on a weighted mechanism to maintain specific pressure levels, ensuring stability.</p><p>4. **Others** encompass various specialized designs catering to unique industry requirements. These valves are crucial for safety and pressure management in various applications.</p></p>
