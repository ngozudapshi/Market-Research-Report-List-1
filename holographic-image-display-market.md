<p><strong>The Holographic Image Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflection Hologram</li><li>Transmission Hologram</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The holographic image display market is primarily divided into two types: reflection holograms and transmission holograms. Reflection holograms utilize light reflecting off the surface of the hologram, creating vivid 3D images that can be viewed under white light. In contrast, transmission holograms require a specific light source to illuminate the hologram from behind, producing detailed images with a depth that changes based on the viewer's angle. Both types have applications in security, art, and display technologies, enhancing visual experiences.</p></p>
