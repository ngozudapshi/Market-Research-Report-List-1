<p><p>Thermoset resin composites are versatile materials known for their durability and heat resistance, primarily used in automotive, aerospace, and construction industries. The Sheet Molding Compound (SMC) is a fiberglass-reinforced resin that is pre-formed into sheets, offering high strength-to-weight ratios and complex shapes. In contrast, Bulk Molding Compound (BMC) is a paste-like mixture of thermosets and fillers, molded into intricate shapes, ideal for high-volume production. Both types enhance product performance and reduce manufacturing costs, catering to diverse applications.</p></p>
