<p><p>Composite heat stabilizers are chemical additives used primarily in the processing of polyvinyl chloride (PVC) to enhance thermal stability and prolong the material’s performance under heat. These stabilizers function by preventing the degradation of PVC when exposed to elevated temperatures during manufacturing and end-use applications. They are increasingly used in various industries due to their ability to provide long-lasting durability and safety in products like pipes, cables, and construction materials.</p><p>The Composite Heat Stabilizer Market is anticipated to expand significantly, projected to grow at a CAGR of 12.1% during the forecast period.
