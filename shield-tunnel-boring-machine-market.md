<p><p>The Shield Tunnel Boring Machine (TBM) market includes various types suited for specific tunneling conditions. Single Shield TBM features a single shield for stability, ideal for soft ground. Double Shield TBM offers enhanced protection and productivity, suitable for challenging environments. Gripper TBM uses hydraulic grippers to anchor against rock for excavation. Slurry TBM manages pressures in unstable soils by using a slurry to support the tunnel face. Other types may include mix-shield and earth pressure balanced TBMs, catering to diverse engineering needs.</p></p>
