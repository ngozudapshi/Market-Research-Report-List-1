<p><p>The Home Use Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) system market is primarily divided into two types: Retrospective CGM and Real-Time Monitoring. Retrospective CGM systems collect glucose data that users review later, often through a connected device, providing insights on trends without immediate alerts. In contrast, Real-Time Monitoring systems deliver continuous glucose readings instantly, alerting users to fluctuations and enabling timely interventions. Both types cater to different user preferences, enhancing diabetes management through improved glucose tracking and insights.</p></p>
