<p><p>Resolution test targets are essential for assessing optical system performance. Star targets allow for testing at various angles, while sinusoidal targets evaluate resolution across different frequencies. Variable frequency targets provide flexibility in resolution testing by adjusting spatial frequency. The USAF pattern wheel target offers a standard way to measure resolving power through distinct patterns. Resolving power charts display the contrast required to discern details at specific resolutions. The IEEE target standardizes testing for imaging systems, ensuring consistent evaluation across platforms.</p></p>
