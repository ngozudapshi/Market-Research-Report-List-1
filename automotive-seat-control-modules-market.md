Manual Seat Control Modules allow users to adjust seating positions manually through levers or knobs, providing basic comfort and ergonomics. In contrast, Memory Seat Control Modules offer advanced features, enabling multiple pre-set seat positions for different drivers, enhancing convenience and personalization.
