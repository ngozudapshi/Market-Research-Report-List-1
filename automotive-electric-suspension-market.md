<p><p>The automotive electric suspension market comprises two primary types: active suspension and semi-active suspension systems. </p><p>Active suspension systems utilize advanced technology to continuously adjust damping characteristics in real time, improving vehicle handling and comfort by actively responding to road conditions. </p><p>In contrast, semi-active suspension systems adjust damping rates using predetermined settings based on sensors, providing a balance between performance and comfort without full active control. Both types enhance ride quality and vehicle stability, crucial for modern automotive design.</p></p>
