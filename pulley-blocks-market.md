<p><p>The Pulley Blocks market is primarily divided into two types: fixed pulleys and moving pulleys. Fixed pulleys are anchored at a single point, allowing users to change the direction of force applied to lift loads, making them ideal for applications requiring minimal effort. Moving pulleys, on the other hand, are attached to the load itself, effectively reducing the amount of force needed to lift heavier objects by distributing weight, making them suitable for lifting heavier loads more efficiently.</p></p>
