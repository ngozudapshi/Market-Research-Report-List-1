<p><p>The Plate and Frame Filter Press is a type of mechanical dewatering equipment used in various industries for solid-liquid separation. It consists of a series of plates and frames stacked together, where the feed slurry is pumped into the spaces between the plates. The liquid then passes through filter cloths, leaving solids trapped in the frames. This technology is widely utilized in sectors such as wastewater treatment, food and beverage, chemicals, and pharmaceuticals due to its efficiency and ability to produce high-quality filtrate.</p><p>The Plate and Frame Filter Press Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.6% during the forecast period.
