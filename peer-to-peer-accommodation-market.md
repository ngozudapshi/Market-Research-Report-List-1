<p><p>The Peer-to-Peer Accommodation market comprises three primary types: Single Room, Private Room, and Entire House/Apartment. Single Rooms are typically shared spaces within a property, offering budget-friendly options for travelers seeking minimal privacy. Private Rooms provide guests with a locked bedroom in a home, combining comfort and a sense of belonging while still maintaining shared common areas. Entire House/Apartment rentals offer complete privacy and independence, catering to larger groups or families who desire home-like amenities during their stay.</p></p>
